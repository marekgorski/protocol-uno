## Template Usage Notes

**When to create this file:**
- First interaction with stakeholder
- When relationship becomes ongoing (3+ interactions expected)

**When to update:**
- After EVERY meeting (capture while fresh)
- After significant DM/email exchange
- When concerns or priorities shift
- When relationship intelligence is learned

**File hygiene:**
- Keep recent meetings (last 5)
- Archive older entries
- Update "Current Context" weekly
- Review "Deliverables & Commitments" on ..gn

**Integration with other files:**
- NEWSFEED.md links to specific meetings here
- UPDATES.md pulls from "Key Points" for stakeholder communications
- TASKS/ drafts reference this person's communication style
